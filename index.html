Coffee Roasts

Starbucks has a variety of roasts customer's can choose from. There most popular roast is Pike, a medium roast preferred by many customers and brewed each day. The usual dark roast is Verona and the usual blonde roast is Veranda. These are also customer favorites and are brewed each day. However, there are occasions where specialty and limited roasts are brewed, such as the famous "Christmas Blend" that is served each year in the month of December. There are Starbucks all around the world totalling over 25,000 different stores today. Starbucks originated in 1971 in Seattle, Washington and has grown to arguably the most well-known coffee shop in the world.

Starbucks Reserve

Starbucks Reserves are less common, but go above and beyond to provide the best coffee experience for each customer. They are small batch, single orgin coffees, not found at normal Starbucks. Some now even serve coffee icecream with an affagotto espresso shot. The inside of these reserves are exquisite, focusing on a serene and hip atmosphere for customers to enjoy their special blend.

  • 500 Reserve Stores in the US
  • 150 in China
  • 90 in Europe
